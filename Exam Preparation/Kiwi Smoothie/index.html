Kiwi Smoothie

Serves 2-3

Total 5 minutes

3 peeleds kiwi fruit

1 chopped mango

500ml pineapple juice

1 sliced banana

This simple kiwi, mango and pineapple fruit smoothie makes an ideal morning treat. It's an easy way to pack in the vitamins and give yourself a boost
